Welcome

About LandOS

LandOS is developed by Gigi Labs to enable a comprehensive approach to land management, combining geospatial intelligence, simulation, and collaborative planning. The Atlas platform integrates data from multiple sources and provides tools for analyzing terrain, infrastructure, and environmental factors to inform decisions and investments.
